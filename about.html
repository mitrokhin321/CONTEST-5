To empower students with cutting-edge technical skills, foster innovation, and build a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to solving real-world problems.

Our Values

Excellence, collaboration, innovation, integrity, and continuous learning guide everything we do. We believe in lifting each other up.

How It All Started

PUCC was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate students who believed that technology education should be more than just classroom lectures. They envisioned a space where students could collaborate, compete, and grow together.

What started as a small group of 20 members meeting in a classroom has evolved into a thriving community of over 600 members across multiple technical disciplines. Today, PUCC stands as a beacon of innovation and excellence at Premier University.

Our journey has been marked by numerous achievements, from championship wins in national competitions to successful placements of our members in top tech companies worldwide. But most importantly, we've created a family of learners who support and inspire each other.

All 6 Specialized Wings

Each wing represents a different facet of technology education and development

CP

Competitive Programming

Mission: Develop problem-solving skills through competitive programming

Members master algorithms, data structures, and optimization techniques. We compete in national and international programming contests.

SE

Software Engineering

Mission: Build enterprise-grade applications with professional practices

Learn full-stack development, system design, and software architecture. Work on real-world projects and contribute to open source.

LN

Linux & Networking

Mission: Master system administration and network protocols

Deep dive into Linux internals, network protocols, cybersecurity, and infrastructure. Develop expertise in system design and deployment.

DNR

Deep Neural Research

Mission: Advance AI and machine learning research

Explore neural networks, deep learning, NLP, and computer vision. Conduct research and contribute to the ML community.

R&D

Research & Development

Mission: Innovate and contribute to technological advancement

Collaborate on cutting-edge research projects, publish papers, and explore emerging technologies.

CG

Career Guidance

Mission: Prepare students for professional success

Industry mentorship, interview preparation, resume building, and placement assistance. Connect with top companies.
